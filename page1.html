The Chronicles of the Celestial Path

In the silent vastness of the night sky, whispers of forgotten constellations echo across the cosmos. Legends speak of a time when the stars themselves danced to the will of mages who once held dominion over light and void alike. Their legacy drifts among the astral winds — glowing runes scattered across galaxies, waiting for someone brave enough to listen.

Every traveler who sets foot on the Celestial Path carries a spark of destiny, a fragment of ancient starlight that binds them to the infinite. It is said that those who walk this road long enough may find the doorway to the First Flame, the original light that gave birth to both time and magic. Many have sought it; few have returned.

But for you, the journey is only beginning. The nebula hums faintly in your chest, and the stars pulse brighter at your name. You raise your gaze to the endless sky, heart steady, and step forward — toward the unknown, toward your own legend.
